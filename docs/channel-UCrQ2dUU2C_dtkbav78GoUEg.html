Despite concerns over increased state intervention, the homeschooling ecosystem has matured. As artificial intelligence dramatically boosts individual productivity, it threatens to displace mid-to-high-tier white-collar roles. Retraining programs struggle to keep pace with these shifts, as government-funded initiatives are structurally slow and often capture benefits for providers rather than workers, ultimately failing to solve structural unemployment.
